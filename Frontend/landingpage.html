About Us

Blixtra is a government services platform designed to make access to various schemes and services seamless and user-friendly. We aim to help citizens of India navigate complex government procedures with ease, offering a fully AI-powered chatbot for assistance.

Our mission is to ensure that everyone, regardless of their digital literacy, has equal access to government resources. Through technology, we hope to empower people by simplifying the processes involved in public service schemes.
